Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how milkry (the “Developer”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the PingMan discord bot (the “Bot”).


Information We Collect

The Bot only collects and stores the following minimal information:

  • User Ids: The Bot collects and stores the unique identifiers of users who interact with the Bot.

  • Channel Ids: The Bot collects and stores the unique identifiers of channels where commands or interactions with the Bot occur.

  • Guild Ids: The Bot collects and stores the unique identifiers of guilds (servers) where commands or interactions with the Bot occur.

  • Commands Executed: The Bot collects and stores information about commands executed, including who executed them, where they were executed, and when they were executed.

  • Feedback Messages: The Bot collects and stores feedback messages submitted by users, including who submitted them and when they were submitted.


How We Use Your Information

The Developer uses the collected information to operate, develop, improve, and promote the Bot. Specifically:

  • User Ids, Channel Ids, and Guild Ids are used to identify users, channels, and guilds for the purpose of providing the intended functionality of the Bot.

  • Commands Executed are used to analyze usage patterns, identify issues or improvements, and enhance the user experience.

  • Feedback Messages are used to gather user feedback, address concerns or suggestions, and improve the Bot’s features.


Information Sharing and Disclosure

The Developer does not share any Bot collected information with third parties.


Data Retention

The Developer retains information collected by the Bot for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. When information is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.


Security

The Developer takes reasonable measures to protect information collected by the Bot from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.


Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information collected by the Bot. You may have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your information. To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries or concerns, please contact the Developer through discord or by using the built-in Bot command /feedback.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Developer reserves the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. Your continued use of or access to the Bot after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact the Developer either through discord or by using the built-in Bot command /feedback.
